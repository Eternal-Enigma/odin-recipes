Fruit Tarts

Fruit tarts are a simple yet elegant dish. They are essentially a mix of your favorite fruits on a thin layer of cream on a short bread cookie base.

Tools You'll Need

    Food Processor (optional)
    Hand or Stand Mixer
    9" or 12" Tart Pan
    Pastry Roller (optional)
    Rubber Spatula
    Scissors
    Bag
    Knife
    Basting Brush

Ingredients

    Flour (All purpose)
    Powdered Sugar
    Granulated Sugar
    Butter
    Cream Cheese
    Sugar
    Vanilla Extract
    Fruit: Kiwi, Blueberries, Strawberries, Rasberries... etc
    Melted Jelly/Preserves

Cooking Instructions

    Add the butter, powdered sugar, white granulated sugar, and flour to a food processor. Pulse until the mixture is well combined and is crumbly.
    Preheat the oven to 350 F (176 C)
    Add the crumbs to the tart pan
    Use clean hands and press down the dough until it is packed and even
    Bake the fruit tart crust in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes until it gets slightly golden brown
    Take the out of oven and allow to cool for 25-30 minutes until completely cooled
    Add the softened cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract to a large bowl and mix with a hand mixer until well combined
    Spread the cream cheese frosting onto the shortbread cookie base, Making it as smooth and as level as possible
    Add the fruit
    You can start in middle and work towards the edges, or use your own design
    Heat the jelly or jam on the stove until melted, and brush over the fruit with a basting brush
    Enjoy!
